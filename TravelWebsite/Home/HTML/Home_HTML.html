About The Website

The Website was created by Nolan Simonds in hope of helping people find where they want to travel for vacations. The Website offers many users the opportunity to understand the opportunities of travel.
Since the websites founding in 2007 it has helped more that 2 million customers find their dream vacation. GoTravel is a hub of travel websites that allows users to find the best experience possible.
With all of these happy customers the Company has been awarded with many awards.

- 2007 Travel Startup of the Year
- 2007 New Travel Company of the Year Award
- 2009 Forbes Travel Company of the Year
- 2010 Jonathan Guo Memorial Travel Website Award
- 2010 Global Tourism Award
- 2011 Radostits Family Worst Website of the Year Award
- 2012 Travel Week Company of the Year
